Enchiladas

Description

We all know it. We all love it. It's enchiladas!

Don't waste another evening forgetting how much you enjoy this tasty treat. Make your loved ones love you back with this easy to make enchiladas recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Corn tortillas
  • Enchilada sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 deg Fahrenheit
  2. Inside each tortilla place chicken thighs, sauce, and sliced red onion
  3. Fold each tortilla and on top layer more sauce and lots of cheese
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Place in oven for 30 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and sprinkle lettuce and more cheese over top.
