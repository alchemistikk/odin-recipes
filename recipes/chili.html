Chili

Description

We all know it. We all love it. It's chili!

Don't waste another evening forgetting how much you enjoy this tasty treat. Make your loved ones love you back with this easy to make chili recipe.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Chicken broth
  • Black beans
  • Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Celery
  • Chili Seasoning
  • Red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot over medium to high heat.
  2. Add ground beef and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken broth, black beans, chopped celery, diced red onion and chili seasonings.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Set heat to low and simmer for several hours.
