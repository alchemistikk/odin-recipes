Pizza

Description

We all know it. We all love it. It's pizza!

Don't waste another evening forgetting how much you enjoy this tasty treat. Make your loved ones love you back with this easy to make pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Bacon
  • Pineapple
  • Red onion
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 deg Fahrenheit
  2. Roll out pizza dough to desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over pizza dough
  4. Sprinkle cheese, pepperoni, chopped bacon, cubed pineapple, and sliced red onion
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Place pizza in oven for 20 minutes until cheese is golden brown
